Key Features

Everything you need to learn French effectively.

Spaced Repetition (SRS)

SM-2 algorithm schedules reviews at optimal intervals for long-term retention. Smart review scheduling adapts to your performance.

Adaptive Difficulty

Per-topic accuracy tracking detects your weak and strong areas, then provides personalized practice recommendations.

Mistake Journal

Automatically records wrong answers with pattern detection — categorizes errors by gender, accent, conjugation, and word order.

Streaks & XP

Earn experience points, track daily streaks, and unlock 14 achievements. Weekly streak freeze lets you miss a day without losing progress.

Text-to-Speech

Hear correct French pronunciation for every phrase and word with adjustable playback speed.

Daily Content

Mot du Jour (90 curated daily words) and cultural notes rotate daily on your home screen to keep learning fresh.

Smart Dashboard

Bento-box widget grid with categorized sections — Core Learning, Reference, and Practice — with badge alerts for due reviews.

Dark Mode

Full dark and light theme support. Clean, modern interface with haptic feedback that feels native on every device.

100% Offline

All content is bundled locally — learn anywhere without an internet connection. On a plane, in the metro, or at a café.
